# CloserAI Overview

CloserAI is an automated sales platform built for freelancers who want to scale their business without spending hours on the phone. By leveraging world-class Voice AI, CloserAI acts as a 24/7 sales representative that knows your services as well as you do.

## Key Value Propositions
- **Save Time:** Let AI handle the first few discovery calls and basic qualification.
- **Better Data:** Get structured reports on every single lead, including specific objections and interest levels.
- **Scalability:** Scale your outreach from 5 calls a day to 500 without hiring a sales team.
- **Premium Experience:** Naturally spoken, human-like voice agents that provide a seamless experience for your potential clients.

## Vision
To bridge the gap between human expertise and automated outreach, allowing freelancers to focus on delivering amazing work while the AI focuses on closing the next big deal.
